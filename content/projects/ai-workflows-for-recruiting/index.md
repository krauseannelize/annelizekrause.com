An end-to-end recruiting automation pipeline built on n8n, where inbound applications flow through six connected workflows: **application intake** (via Tally forms), **AI classification**, **CV extraction** to structured fields, **phone capture** via webhook (using ngrok for local tunneling), **structured job intake** with skill normalization, and **candidate-job matching** with AI-based fit scoring plus automated alerts.

Built as the capstone for Masterschool's AI Productivity module. The project demonstrates how AI can take over high-volume, structured recruiting work, freeing the human to focus on judgment-heavy decisions.
